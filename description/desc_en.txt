Tab Bar adds a document tab bar to LibreOffice Writer. All open Writer documents appear as clickable tabs in a toolbar — switch between documents instantly without using the Window menu.

Features:
  • Active tab highlighted with ● indicator
  • Modified (unsaved) documents marked with *
  • Per-tab menu: Rename, Save, Save As, Move Left/Right, New Document, Close, Close All Others, Close All
  • Saved Tab Sets: save a group of open documents and restore them in one click
  • Restore Last Session: reopen the documents you had open when you last quit
  • Optional Ctrl+Tab / Ctrl+Shift+Tab cycling (toggle in the Sets menu)
  • Works on Windows, macOS, and Linux
